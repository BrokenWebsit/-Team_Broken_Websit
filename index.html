Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is this service completely free?

Yes! LovePro is completely free to use with no hidden costs or premium tiers. We believe expressing love shouldn't come with a price tag.

Can I use this for anniversaries or other occasions?

Absolutely! While designed for proposals, many users have successfully adapted it for anniversaries, Valentine's Day messages, or just to express their feelings on special occasions.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy. All processing happens locally in your browser, and we don't store any personal information on our servers. The generated links don't contain sensitive data.

What if the person says no?

While we hope for positive responses, we've designed the experience to be gentle and respectful regardless of the outcome. The "maybe later" option provides a graceful way to respond without pressure.

Can I customize the proposal after creating it?

Yes, you can always go back and modify your proposal before sharing the link. Once shared, changes will be reflected immediately for anyone with the link.
